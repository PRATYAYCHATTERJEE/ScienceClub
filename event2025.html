NATIONAL ENERGY CONSERVATION DAY 14TH DECEMBER, 2025

The National Energy Conservation Day is celebrated on December 14 every year in our country in order to spread the message on the importance of conserving energy and to showcase the achievements of the nation in energy efficiency and conservation. This year it was celebrated in our college on 08th January, 2026 due to university examination. This day was celebrated through SAVE ENERGY TODAY AND SECURE TOMORROW USING GREEN TECH POWERING SUSTAINABILITY. Students of Asansol Engineering College participated in the idea competition.

SAVE ENERGY TODAY AND SECURE TOMORROW USING GREEN TECH POWERING SUSTAINABILITY.

The Science Club of Asansol Engineering College had organized an PPT, PROJECT(Hardware/Software/Science) making and Video Creation competition for the Students of Asansol Engineering College, Asansol namely PPT and POSTER Competition on National Energy Conservation Day, 14th December 2025. Near about 50 students (14 teams) participated from various departments of Asansol Engineering College. The topic of the program is “SAVE ENERGY TODAY AND SECURE TOMORROW USING GREEN TECH POWERING SUSTAINABILITY”.
